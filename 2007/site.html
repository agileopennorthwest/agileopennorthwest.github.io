About This Site

This site was created by Charlie Poole and is hosted by Poole Consulting. Graphics assistance was provided by Josh Blank and Phil Brock.

Our logo is adapted with permission from that of the Agile Open event held in Belgium for the past two years.

The main site uses PHP, HTML and CSS. It's intentionally kept very simple.

The Wiki is a slightly modified copy of UseMod. Most of the changes in appearance were handled by CSS but a few of them required modifying the Perl script that generates HTML elements.

Our registration uses sporg.com.
